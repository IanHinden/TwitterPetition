Privacy Policy

Last Updated 12/14/2020

What Information Of Yours We Collect

  • E-mail Address
  • Twitter Username
  • Unique Twitter ID
  • Follower Count
  • Status as a verified user
  • Date and time of the creation of your petition account
  • Twitter profile picture URL
  • Current status (signed or unsigned) of petition
  • Whether we have permission to send e-mails
  • Whether we have permission to display your account to other users

Where we get this data

This data is collected when you click the "Login with Twitter" button and is provided to us, with your permission, from Twitter as well as input from you once you are logged in to your account.

What we do with this data

  • E-mail address: Used to send you updates. You can opt out of receiving e-mails on your profile.
  • Twitter Username: Used to periodically check your counnt to update the follower totals. Your username can also be displayed when we recommend users to each other.
  • Unique Twitter ID: Used as a unique identifier in our database.
  • Follower Count: Used as the count for petition signees.
  • Status as a verified user: Used to determine which category, verified or non-verified, for displaying top contributors.
  • Date and time of the creation of your petition account: Used for data analysis to study the spread of the petition.
  • Twitter profile picture URL: Used for display as a top contributor or recommended user.
  • Current status (signed or unsigned) of petition: Used to determine whether follower count is added to total.
  • Whether we have permission to send e-mails: Used in determing whether to send e-mails or not.
  • Whether we have permission to display your account to other users: Used in determining whether to display user as contributor or recommended user.

Where we share your data

Your profile can be recommended to other users randomly or as a top contributor. You may opt out of being recommended in your profile.

What rights you have over your data

You can update your profile at any time to sign or unsign the petition, receive or opt out of e-mails, or opt-in or opt-out of being displayed to others. You can also delete your account entirely by contacting us at the contact information below.

Children's Policy

We do not solicit information or market to chuldren under the age of 13. If you have become aware of any information we have collected from a child under 13 years of age, please contact us at the address below.
